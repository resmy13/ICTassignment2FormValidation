About Us

Kerala Tourism Department

The Kerala Tourism Department is your gateway to an enchanting and unforgettable journey through "God's Own Country." Our mission is to showcase the myriad wonders of Kerala, a land blessed with pristine landscapes, rich traditions, and warm hospitality. Imagine serene houseboat cruises through the picturesque backwaters of Alappuzha, tranquil moments on the sun-kissed beaches of Kovalam, or thrilling encounters with wildlife in the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. Kerala offers all this and more. Our department is committed to making your visit seamless and memorable.


As you embark on your Kerala journey, our tourism department stands ready to assist you in every way. We're here to ensure your visit is not just a vacation but an enriching experience.
